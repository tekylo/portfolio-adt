# STITCH PROMPT - AdTech Portfolio

We're building a unique AdTech portfolio where the entire site mimics a digital newspaper ("El Diario AdTech") with fake ad modules. These aren't real ads—they're creative contact/info cards designed to LOOK like real AdTech advertising from Google Ad Manager, but they're actually portfolio showcases.

## WHAT MAKES THIS SPECIAL
This portfolio is built BY an AdTech professional FOR AdTech professionals. The "ads" are tongue-in-cheek - they simulate what real ad tech looks like (sizes, labels, layouts from Google Ad Manager) but showcase the portfolio owner's skills and contact info.

## AD SIZES (IAB Standard)
- Billboard: 970x250 (hero CV ad)
- Medium Rectangle: 300x250 (tech stack)
- Leaderboard: 728x90 (CTA campaign)
- Wide Skyscraper: 160x600 (contact sidebar)
- Skins: 300x600 (tech badges)

## DESIGN SYSTEM
**Linear-inspired dark mode:**
- Background: #08090a (near black)
- Secondary: #0f1011
- Borders: rgba(255,255,255,0.06) ultra-subtle
- Text primary: #f7f8f8
- Text secondary: #d0d6e0
- **Accent: GOLD #f3d34d** (our signature - NOT indigo/purple)
